Abstract

This study examines the determinants of legal cooperative adoption among cannabis farmers in Morocco's Rif region following the enactment of Law 13-21 (2021). Using a household survey of 800 farmers across Chefchaouen, Al-Hoceima, and Ouazzane provinces, we estimate logistic regression and propensity score matching models to identify barriers to formalization and quantify the income effect of joining the legal market.
